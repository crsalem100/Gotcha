Our Story

BORN AND RAISED
IN EUGENE

Gotcha Burger was born from a family tradition. Trevon Huntley grew up in Eugene watching his father perfect a burger and sauce recipe that would one day become legendary. Surrounded by diverse, home-cooked flavors — from Indian to Mediterranean — one meal always stood apart: his dad's burgers and fries.

What started as a family recipe at the Kazaam food truck on East 18th Street quickly became their best-selling item. So the Huntleys did what any passionate family would do — they went all in and opened Gotcha Burger.

Today, Trevon runs day-to-day operations, working from open to close — chopping fresh vegetables, forming patties by hand, and building relationships with every customer who rolls through.

"We call it Gotcha Burger because we gotcha — whether you're a student looking for a quick bite, a family from the top of the hill, or just someone needing a late-night meal."

— Trevon Huntley, Founder
